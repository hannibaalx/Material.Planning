Replenishment Station Guidelines:
  • Distribution centers (defined as D00, CLT, PHX and TUL) replenish themselves for all M&Es
  • PIT replenishes itself for all M&Es due to the base activity
  • Boeing (737, 777, and 787) replenish out of TUL/DFW
    • Leave TUL/DFW as currently allocated in system until AA1 is built
    • Change to AA1 after it is in operation
    • MCLA 777 replenishes from M01
  • Airbus (A320F)
    • East – Fill from CLT
    • West – Fill from PHX
    • Central – Fill from D00
  • Multi-fleet
    • East – Fill from CLT
    • West – Fill from PHX
    • Central – Fill from D00
  • Exceptions
    • M&Es with TUL home shop are distributed out of TUL
    • Class 1 stations will replenish themselves for expendable hazmat M&Es
